🌿 About the Project

Crop Disease Detection System

A deep learning powered web application that identifies diseases in potato and tomato leaf images — giving farmers and researchers instant diagnosis, confidence scores, treatment advice, and downloadable PDF reports.  फसल रोग पहचान AI प्रणाली।

⚡
Features
📷
Camera Capture
Take leaf photos directly from your device camera without saving a file first.
🤖
AI Prediction
Custom CNN models predict the disease class with a confidence score for each image.
📋
PDF Report
Download a full diagnosis report with disease description and treatment plan.
📊
Live Dashboard
Separate dashboards for Potato, Tomato, and Overall — auto-refreshed every 4 seconds.
🕐
Test History
Every prediction stored with timestamp, confidence score, and crop type.
💊
Treatment Info
Bilingual (English + Hindi) disease description and treatment for every class.
🧠
AI Models Used
🥔 Potato

Custom CNN — Potato Model

Custom Convolutional Neural Network trained from scratch on the PlantVillage potato dataset with baked-in preprocessing.

Architecture: Custom CNN
Input: 256 × 256 px
Classes: 3
Layers: 4× Conv + GAP + Dense
Rescaling: Baked 1/255
🍅 Tomato

Custom CNN — Tomato Model V2

Same custom CNN architecture trained on the PlantVillage tomato dataset. Identifies 10 disease classes including viral and fungal conditions.

Architecture: Custom CNN
Input: 256 × 256 px
Classes: 10
Layers: 4× Conv + GAP + Dense
Rescaling: Baked 1/255

⚙️
How It Works
1
Select Crop Type
Choose Potato or Tomato — this loads the correct trained model.
2
Upload or Capture Leaf Image
Upload a saved photo or use the live camera to capture the leaf directly from your device.
3
Image Preprocessing
Image is resized to 256×256 px and passed as raw pixels — the model's baked Rescaling(1/255) layer normalises internally.
4
CNN Inference
Forward pass through 4 Conv layers → Global Average Pooling → Dense layers → Softmax probabilities across all classes.
5
Result + Treatment
Top predicted class, confidence score, bilingual disease description, and treatment advice shown on the result page.
6
Stored & Tracked
Every test saved to the database — reflected instantly on the live dashboard and history page.
